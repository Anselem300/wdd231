Purpose of Mental Lab

Focuses on mental wellness tips, support resources, and outreach. The website will focus on mental health awareness, providing users with educational content, self-care tools, and access to relevant support resources. The site aims to promote mental well-being and break the stigma surrounding mental health issues by offering clear, accessible, and empathetic information in a user-friendly way.

Scenarios

What is the best way to deal with and manage mental stress?

Where can I find substantial information on what steps to take when battling mental illness?
